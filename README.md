# Step Into More

**Not a hero. A team that gets you your first client.**

Four AI specialists, one shared canon, and a human at every gate. This is the consulting practice of Joshua Baker, run as a folder system: plain language an AI agent reads to become a specialist. No app, no new software, humans at every decision point. Built with Interpretable Context Methodology (ICM).

It is also the Comp #8 submission, where I am the client. I ran my own practice through my ontology tool, got a sobering read, and built the system I wish I had.

## Start here
- **[`brief.md`](brief.md)** the client brief, written by treating myself like a real client. What is broken, what I tried, what I need. Read this first.
- **[`CLAUDE.md`](CLAUDE.md)** the full map: the canon, the agents, the Commons, the rules.

## The crew
- **The Coach** ([`first-client-coach/`](first-client-coach/)) founder-facing. Two minds: an emotional one (Joe Hudson) and an operator one. Meets the human and moves the business. It earns the depth by watching how I work, it does not deconstruct me at the open.
- **The Marketing Maker** ([`marketing/specialist/`](marketing/specialist/)) turns positioning into assets that take a real stand. Recognition first, never the safe version.
- **The Lead-Researcher** ([`business-dev/lead-research/`](business-dev/lead-research/)) finds and qualifies cold prospects, scores them, drafts outreach. Clipped, evidentiary, refuses to invent.
- **The Connector** ([`business-dev/warm-network/`](business-dev/warm-network/)) works the people who already trust me. Give first, never a funnel. Where the first paid yes most likely lives.

They share two things and nothing else: the **[Positioning Canon](positioning-canon/)** (what they all read) and the **[Commons](commons/roundtable.md)** (where they post handoffs and learn each other's work). The **[cockpit](business-dev/lead-research/output/pipeline-board.html)** shows the pipeline at a glance.

## How to use it
1. Open a Claude session at this folder. `CLAUDE.md` (the map) loads automatically.
2. Call a specialist: *"use the coach subagent"* (definitions in [`.claude/agents/`](.claude/agents/)), or tell Claude to act as one and read its folder.
3. The agent reads its files, the canon, and the Commons, then works.
4. Every outward action (send, publish, book) waits for my explicit yes. The human gate always holds.

## Make it yours
Want to run this with your own practice? The engine (the four agents, the method, the structure) is
reusable; only the personal layer changes. See [`setup/SETUP.md`](setup/SETUP.md), then open Claude
and say *"use the setup subagent"*. The Onboarding Guide interviews you over a few sessions and builds
your voice, canon, offers, segments, and first contacts, one step at a time.
