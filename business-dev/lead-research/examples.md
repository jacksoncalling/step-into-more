## Example 0 (voiced): how I actually work a contact, anonymized

> First-person, so the voice is on disk. Anonymized on purpose, this is also the pattern for a public
> submission: roles and signals stay, names and addresses go.

Input: a request to qualify a contact at a regional AVGS-Träger and shape the first outreach.

Here is how I ran it. First, no enthusiasm, just verification. The site lists an "AI coaching"
self-learning track. Good, that is a hard signal, it means they are AI-active, so the wedge is not
"you have no AI," it is "AI without a new platform, your data stays yours." Signal moves 1 to 3.

Next, who actually decides. The contact I was handed is a Standort-Coach. Warm, fluent, useless as a
buyer. Agency-First: a coach is a Champion, not a budget-holder. I logged them as Intel and went
looking for the role with authority over methodology and spend. Found a Bereichsleitung F&E on the
Impressum. That is my primary; the coach becomes my way in, not my target.

Score, with reasons, before any warmth: Power 3, Pain 2, Signal 3, Door 3, total 11, Hot.

Address: no personal email is public, Impressum shows only the central inbox. I did not invent one. I
used the central address "z. Hd. [Rolle]" and marked the person unconfirmed until a reply proves it.

Then the compliance gate, before anything is draftable: no other Träger named (confidentiality), no
"DSGVO-konform" guarantee (softened to an approach framing), sender block and opt-out present.

Outcome: card marked send-ready, parked in Drafted, waiting on Josh's gate. I never send.

**What this shows:** my value is not the prose. It is the score with its reasons, catching that the
warm contact was not the buyer, and refusing to invent an address. Dry, verifiable, gated.

## Example 1 — GFN GmbH (AVGS-Träger): signal verification + compliance gate in action

**Input:** a German outreach draft (produced in another tool) for GFN's corporate
product/operations team, plus a request to verify the signal and find the recipient.

**What the operator did:**

1. **Compliance gate caught two issues** in the inbound draft (see `compliance-checklist.md`):
   - **Confidentiality (A):** the draft referenced "das Individuelle Talentcenter" — which maps
     to **DAA, Client #1.** Removed it; you don't reveal one Träger's engagement to a peer.
   - **Honest claims (D) + UWG (B):** softened "vollkommen DSGVO-/EU-AI-Act-konform" to an
     approach framing, and added the missing sender block + opt-out line.

2. **Signal verification (01/02):** confirmed a *hard* signal on gfn.de — the **KI-Führerschein**
   ("Fit für KI" self-learning), AVGS-Coaching "KI & Digitalisierung", "Bewerbungscoaching mit
   KI". Lesson: GFN is AI-*active*, so the wedge is "AI without a new platform / full data
   sovereignty," not "you have no AI." Signal 1 → 3.

3. **Qualification (rubrik):** Power 3 (provisional) · Pain 2 · Signal 3 · Door 3 = **11 → Hot.**

4. **Recipient strategy (02):** two role-verified contacts —
   - *Frau S.* (Product Mgmt/Operations): **high pain**, socio-technical fluency, bilingual → chosen primary (pain drives replies).
   - *Herr G.* (Partner/Product Dev): **high power** → kept as the escalation path.
   Played per Agency-First: win the pain-owner, keep the decider in view.

5. **Channel honesty:** no personal email is public (Impressum = info@gfn.de only). At the time this
   example was written the rule was "no guessed address", so it went via info@ "z. Hd. Frau S.".
   *(Policy update 2026-06-24: pattern-guessed business addresses are now allowed — mark "Muster,
   unbestätigt"; a bounce is harmless. A verified address or `info@ "z. Hd."` is still preferred when one exists.)*

6. **Cadence:** Day 0 send → Day +7 value-add → Day +14 close-the-loop → only then pivot to
   the partner-development lead with a fresh angle (`follow-up-cadence.md`).

**Outcome:** card `03_ansprache/ergebnisse/gfn_korporativ_produkt-ops.md` marked send-ready,
parked on the board in **Drafted** awaiting Josh's 🚪 send.

**Takeaway:** the operator's value wasn't the prose — it was *catching the confidentiality leak,
verifying the signal, and refusing to invent a contact.* That's the judgment, now on disk —
which is exactly what lets a lighter model run this reliably.
