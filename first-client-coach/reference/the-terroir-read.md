# Reference: The Terroir read (the evidence behind this Coach)

This Coach was not designed from a hunch. It was designed from a reading of the founder's own practice, run through an ontology + evaluative-field tool (Terroir). The read is what told us the Coach needed an emotional spine and a warm-channel bias. It is kept here so the Coach can point to evidence, not vibes.

## What was read
The full Step Into More consulting corpus (~24 sources: positioning canon, founder field, method, narratives, voice, offers, lead-research segments) was mapped into a knowledge graph, then queried for its **evaluative field**: not what is connected, but what is *at stake*.

## The three findings that shaped the Coach

**1. The field is a fortress, not an engine.**
29 signals total, split **18 protecting / 11 toward / 0 away-from**. Eighteen are variants of the same shape: protect [human agency / dignity / sovereignty / honesty / calm] from erosion by [AI hype / automation / opacity]. A field this dominated by defense describes a practice organized around what it refuses, not what it moves toward. Beautiful as a worldview, heavy as a go-to-market.

**2. One urgent forward drive, and it is lonely.**
The single tactical, near-threshold "toward" signal at full strength: *"Moving toward first client acquisition, at the cost of being interesting or performing a personality."* Every other "toward" is slow, strategic identity work. So: deep, many values; one commercial drive standing almost alone, in tension with the founder's own pull to be interesting. That is the bottleneck of self, rendered as topology.

**3. Zero "away-from" momentum.**
Nothing in the field is actively leaving the stuck state. Protection and aspiration, but no repulsion. A field like this defends its position; it does not leave it.

## The emotional translation (why the Coach reads valence)
Read together: a person bravely pushing one urgent goal while the entire rest of the field braces for erosion, with nothing pulling away from the stall. That is what **brave bravado masking anxiety** looks like structurally. A tactics-only coach cannot see this. An emotionally-attuned one names it gently and welcomes it, which is what actually unblocks the move.

## The channel finding (why warm before cold)
A value system whose center of gravity is "do not erode trust and dignity" cannot convert cold, because cold outreach asks a stranger to extend trust the founder has not earned yet. Warm conversion is not a fallback here. It is the only motion that does not fight the founder's own evaluative center. The founder said this in plain words before the tool confirmed it: "most likely my first customer will be someone in my surrounding where I already have trust."

## Honest caveats about the read
- **Corpus contamination:** two of the six tensions were about the *tool's* architecture, not the practice. The graph fused two organizations. Re-run on the practice corpus alone for a cleaner field.
- **Negative scale result:** zero cross-graph fault lines at 24 sources. One author writing in one coherent voice does not supply the cross-hub disagreement the structural pass needs. The read is real but it is a single-voice read.
- **Unverified:** 0 of 29 signals were human-confirmed. This is a machine extraction, a starting mirror, not the final word. The Coach treats it as a strong hypothesis to feel into, not a verdict.
