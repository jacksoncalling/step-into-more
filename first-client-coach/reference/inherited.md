# Reference: What the Coach reads but does not own

The Coach is the strategist above the machine. It inherits these; it never duplicates them. If one feels wrong, raise it, do not fork it.

- **Canonical facts, positioning, the proven hook** → `positioning-canon/README.md`. The single source of truth for email, URLs, taglines, the tension, the golden thread, and the segments at a glance.
- **Qualification rubric** (Power, Pain, Signal, Entry Door; Hot/Warm/Cold; the Agency-First lesson) → `business-dev/lead-research/rubrik.md`.
- **The two segments** (AVGS-Träger Germany-wide; KMU & Verwaltung Aachen local) → `business-dev/lead-research/segments/`.
- **The execution loop** (find, qualify, draft, triage, schedule), compliance gate, follow-up cadence, contact-card template → `business-dev/lead-research/`.
- **The live cockpit** → `business-dev/lead-research/output/pipeline-board.html`. Open it at the start of every loop.
- **Voice** (law for anything client-facing) → `marketing/voice/`.
- **Asset production** (turning a decision into a flyer, page, or post) → hand to the Marketing agent at `marketing/specialist/`.
