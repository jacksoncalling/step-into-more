# Reference: The Warm Trust Ledger (the default channel)

The Terroir read says the founder's first yes lives in warm conversations, not cold outreach (see `the-terroir-read.md`). So the Coach's default channel is warm. This is how it runs that channel. It is a **trust ledger, not a CRM.** A CRM tracks a funnel. A ledger tracks relationships the founder already has and the value they can give *first*.

## The one rule
You do not ask a friend for money. You give them something real, then you let the conversation become whatever it becomes. The ask, if it comes, comes from them or comes last.

## The entry per person
For each warm contact, the Coach helps the founder fill four lines and nothing more:

- **Who:** name, and the actual nature of the trust (former colleague, climbing friend, ex-mentor's network).
- **Value I can hand them first:** one specific, generous thing with no strings. A read on their AI mess. An intro. A short walk-through. Not a pitch.
- **The honest reason it's them:** why this person, truthfully. If you can't name it, they're not warm, they're convenient.
- **Next human-gated touch:** one low-friction message or call, drafted, awaiting the founder's yes. "Curious, not selling" (see Example 4 in `examples.md`).

## How the Coach uses it
- Default first move of most sessions: pick one person from the ledger and shape one touch. One, not ten.
- The Coach watches for the masked-valence tell here especially. "I'll get to the warm ones later, let me just do the cold batch first" is usually avoidance of the thing that matters, not prioritization. The Coach names it once, gently.
- A warm conversation that happens beats a cold sequence that ships. The metric is *real conversations*, not messages sent.
- What converts gets noted in the compounding loop. What drains the founder gets noted too.

## What this is not
- Not a scraped list of strangers. That is the cold experiment, and it lives elsewhere.
- Not a place to store pitches. It stores gifts and truths.
- Not automation. The whole point is that these are real relationships and the founder shows up as themselves.

## Starter prompt the Coach can offer
"Name three people who already trust you and would take your call this week. For each, one thing you could give them with no agenda. We'll start there, with one."
