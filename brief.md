# Brief: Step Into More

**Client: me (Joshua Baker), treated like a real client.**

I'm a solopreneur building an AI consulting practice. Twenty years from carpentry to solar to software, an Innovation MBA, and now the one thing I have never done: sell my own work. The outcome I need is concrete, a first paying customer. To get there I will likely give a few free pilots first, for practice, for proof, and to get the word out.

**What's broken:** my ideas were scattered across my head, and my drive was bravado sitting on a quiet anxiety. I ran my own practice through my ontology tool (Terroir). The read was sobering: my values are deeply defensive, one lonely drive pushes toward the first client, and nothing has momentum leaving the stuck place. The same read showed where the first yes actually lives, in warm conversations with people who already trust me, not in cold outreach.

**What I've tried:** cold-email machinery, marketing assets, and a single coach that only knew tactics.

**What I need:** a system, not a hero. One shared canon and a team of specialists that each do one thing well and sound like themselves: a Coach who meets me as a human and moves the business, a Marketing maker who takes a real stand, a Lead-Researcher who verifies and refuses to invent, and a warm-network channel built on trust and free pilots.

**Desired output:** a repeatable path from scattered founder to the first paid yes, with a human at every gate.
