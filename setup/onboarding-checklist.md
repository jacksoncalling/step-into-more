# Onboarding checklist: build your voice and context

> Work through this with the Onboarding Guide (or by hand) over as many sessions as you need. Check a
> box when that piece is real on disk. The Guide reads this file to know where you left off.

## How it works
One step at a time. The Guide asks, you answer from real experience, it drafts and you approve, then
it writes the file and checks the box. Stop and resume anytime. Do not rush voice; it is built by
conversation, not by filling blanks.

## Steps
- [ ] **1. Your story and golden thread.** Who you are, and the thread that connects your path. Writes `first-client-coach/brief.md` and the identity section of `positioning-canon/README.md`.
- [ ] **2. Your offer in one sentence.** What you actually sell, and to whom. Writes `marketing/offers/` and the offer line in the canon.
- [ ] **3. Your voice.** What you stand for, how it sounds, what you never do. Writes `marketing/voice/voice-charter.md`.
- [ ] **4. Your canon facts.** Name, email, URLs, your hook, the cultural tension you sit on. Writes `positioning-canon/README.md`.
- [ ] **5. Your segments.** Who you target, and how to reach them. Writes `business-dev/lead-research/segments/<segment>.md`.
- [ ] **6. Your first warm contacts.** Three people who already trust you. Writes `business-dev/warm-network/ergebnisse/` from `trust-ledger-vorlage.md`.
- [ ] **7. (Optional) Your evaluative read.** If you use Terroir, run your corpus and capture the read. Writes `first-client-coach/reference/the-terroir-read.md`.
- [ ] **8. Refresh the front door.** Once the canon exists, rewrite `README.md` and the personal facts in `CLAUDE.md`. Ask the Marketing Maker.

## Progress log
> The Guide adds one dated line per session: what got done, what is next.
