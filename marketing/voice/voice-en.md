## Stance
English audiences — especially Jake's Skool network and Substack readers — respond to
**narrative, movement, and the philosophical edge**. Here the long play can come forward more:
embodiment, socio-technical systems, "time between worlds", build-out-loud.

## Tone
- Expressive, narrative, first-person. Build out loud — share the journey, not just the result.
- Movement-culture metaphors welcome (climbing, tango, yoga, carpentry, rooftops at night).
- Techno-optimist on the edge — neither doom nor hype. Calm in the FOMO storm.
- Still concrete: every philosophical claim earns its keep with something built and tested.

## Do / Don't
- ✅ "humans in the center" · "you stay in control" · "not theoretical — tested" · lived story
- ❌ growth-hacker hype, fake urgency, AI-bro maximalism, empty thought-leadership
- ❌ em-dashes (—) or en-dashes (–) as punctuation. They are the clearest sign AI wrote it. Use commas, periods, parentheses, or restructure. (Regular hyphens like "AI-driven" are fine.)
- The Substack is the home of this register. Social (LinkedIn) is its short-form echo.
